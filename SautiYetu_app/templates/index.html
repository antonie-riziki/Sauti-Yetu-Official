Our Products

Assistive technology solutions designed for people with visual and speech impairments.

Sightra

Sightra helps people with visual impairment or blindness navigate independently using a smart cane and web-based guidance. AI interprets surroundings and provides voice instructions in real-time for safe mobility.

Theravoo

Theravoo assists people with speech impairments to communicate effectively. AI interprets non-standard speech and provides contextual understanding for caregivers, therapists, and digital platforms.
